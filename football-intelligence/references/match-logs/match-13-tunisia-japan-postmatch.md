**New Heuristic:** If manager is hired <7 days before a tournament match, treat the team as functionally 1 tier LOWER in quality for that match. Tunisia went from "weak but organized" → "chaotic, demoralized, non-functional."

### LESSON 5: Correct-Score Blowout Tail (Rule #34 Formalization)

**Finding:** My Bivariate Poisson model is calibrated for typical mismatches (quality gap +10 to +15 pts). For extreme mismatches (gap >25 pts), it UNDERSHOOTS on blowout scores (3–4 goal margins).

**Calibration Insight #10:** The model FAILED to assign sufficient probability to 4-goal-margin outcomes for extreme quality gaps. **New Rule #34 — Blowout Score Tail (Formal):**

- When quality gap <15 pts: Use standard Bivariate Poisson (no adjustment)

**Implementation:** After running Bivariate Poisson, redistribute probability:
- 1-goal margins: −5 pts
- 2-goal margins: −10 pts
- 3-goal margins: +8 pts
- 4-goal margins: +7 pts

  - Add +2–3% to favorite's win probability
