# World Cup 2026 Team Profiles — Starter Database

Purpose: store current World Cup team mechanisms as they are observed. This is a starter database, not a complete scouting database. Unknowns must remain unknown until verified with stats, heatmaps, lineups, or credible reports.

## Brazil

Status: Observed

Recent results:

- Brazil 1-1 Morocco
- Brazil 3-0 Haiti

Current read:

- Brazil's attacking ceiling improved when Matheus Cunha provided central finishing movement.
- Vinicius Junior remains the main chaos creator and chance-quality accelerator.
- The 3-0 over Haiti was more clinical than territorially dominant based on shared match stats: possession 57%, shots 9-8, shots on target 5-4, corners 4-4.

Strengths:

- Elite individual chance conversion.
- Left-side threat through Vinicius.
- High finishing quality when central finisher is active.

Risks:

- Possession/control may be less dominant than scoreline suggests.
- Opponents can still create shots and corners.
- Do not over-upgrade chance creation from Haiti scoreline alone.

Model modifier:

- Upgrade Brazil finishing quality.
- Do not heavily upgrade Brazil shot-volume dominance without more evidence.

## Germany

Status: Observed

Recent results:

- Germany 7-1 Curacao
- Germany 2-1 Ivory Coast

Current read:

- Germany have elite attacking structure and bench finishing impact.
- The Curacao match was a blowout but should be opponent-adjusted.
- Ivory Coast exposed transition risk and proved Germany are not immune to athletic counters.

Strengths:

- High chance creation.
- Intricate final-third combinations.
- Strong substitute/central finisher impact through Deniz Undav.

Risks:

- High line can be attacked.
- Wirtz/Musiala chance creation does not always equal Wirtz scoring.
- Against athletic teams, domination may be moderate, not overwhelming.

Model modifier:

- Upgrade Germany win probability and chance creation.
- Keep transition-concession tail active.
- Separate creator roles from finisher roles.
